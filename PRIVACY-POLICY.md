# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-05-26

Decimal (the "App") respects your privacy.

## 1. Information We Collect

The App runs primarily on your Mac and does not require account registration.

By default:

- We do not upload your file contents to our servers.
- We do not require you to create an online account.
- We do not collect personally identifying information to provide the core functionality.

## 2. Files and Local Data

Files, folder structures, index data, and local settings you manage in the App are primarily stored on your device.

The App is designed to be local-first, and the file system is the primary source of truth.

## 3. AI Features

If you explicitly enable AI classification or related intelligent features:

- file names, partial paths, and prompts you enter may be sent to the third-party AI provider you choose;
- file contents are not sent by default unless you explicitly use a feature that clearly requires content analysis and it is disclosed in the interface;
- your API key is stored locally in your system Keychain and is not sent to our servers.

How third-party AI providers process data is governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

## 4. Calendar Integration

If you enable calendar-related features, the App may access Apple Calendar after receiving system permission in order to create or sync items you explicitly manage in the App.

## 5. Data Sharing

Except where necessary to provide third-party integrations that you explicitly enable, the App does not sell, rent, or share your data with advertising platforms.

## 6. Data Security

We use reasonable efforts to rely on macOS security mechanisms for local storage of settings and credentials, including the system Keychain where applicable. You are also responsible for securing your device, account, and backups.

## 7. Your Choices

You may at any time:

- disable AI features;
- avoid configuring any third-party API key;
- delete local data or uninstall the App;
- revoke system permissions such as Calendar access.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time through app updates or the public page where it is published. The latest version is indicated by the effective date above.
